Community-Based
Gunshot Alert System

The Civic Alert project links community engagement with advanced technology to address gun violence. By deploying a network of inexpensive, resident-hosted acoustic sensors, we provide a scalable, community-controlled solution for detecting and localizing illegal gunfire. In partnership with the North Austin Civic Association (NACA), this pilot empowers residents with real-time information, improving safety, peace of mind, and the effectiveness of law enforcement response.

How It Works

1

Community Sensing

Residents host small, privacy-preserving acoustic sensors that simply plug into a standard outlet. These sensors detect loud impulsive sounds like gunfire while ignoring normal street noise.

2

Intelligent Verification

Potential detections are securely analyzed using machine learning to filter out fireworks and backfires. Data from multiple sensors is fused to pinpoint the exact location.

3

Instant Alerts

Qualified alerts are immediately sent to community members via the mobile app, empowering residents with real-time safety information and peace of mind.

Mobile App-Based Monitoring and Alerts

Receive instant notifications when gunfire is detected in your neighborhood. The app provides real-time information, allowing you to make informed decisions and enhancing community situational awareness.

Crowd-Sourced Localization

Our system uses a distributed network of sensors to triangulate the source of gunfire with high precision. By fusing data from multiple community-hosted devices, we can distinguish between actual threats and false alarms, providing reliable data to residents and first responders.

Privacy & Community Trust

Designed ensuring resident privacy and control.

Privacy First

No continuous audio recording. The system only processes audio on-device and transmits short, encrypted evidence clips only when a gunshot is positively detected.

Community Owned

Unlike other systems, this project is community-centered. Residents host the sensors and own the data, fostering trust and transparency.

Secure Technology

Utilizing Thingstream IoT connectivity and AWS cloud infrastructure ensures reliable, secure, and fast data transmission without accessing your home WiFi.
